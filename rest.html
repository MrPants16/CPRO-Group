Resting

Resting is an essential to maintain a positive and healthy lifestyle. Without it, you are at risk of having an inability to regulating your emotions, lack in cognitive ability and it can have physical and mental effects on your health.

To ensure you are resting enough to maintain good health, you should have a consistent sleep schedule and ensure your sleep environmet is dark, cool and comfortable.

Benefits from adequate sleep each night.

  1. Enhanced brain function.
  2. Stronger immune system.
  3. Assists in cardiovascular health.
  4. Reduces the risk of chronic diseases.
  5. Increased energy and you are more alert.

Negative impacts that lack of sleep can have on your life.

  • Increased risk of hypertension.
  • Increased risk of diabetes.
  • Increased risk of depression.
  • Higher blood pressure.
  • Have less energy and motivation to do anything. Feeling more drowsy.
