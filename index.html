Putting in hour after hour with less than ideal posture as we crinkle our brow, upon reading the most recent bug report. Nutrition

When considering the steps we can actively take to work on and improve our health, nutrition is simultaneously the easiest and most difficult change to put into practice. Considering both sides of that double-edged sword, nutrition is extremely easy to implement, as it can be as simple as monitoring and adjusting the products we consume on a daily basis. Ironically, this is also how nutrition can be the most difficult: it's not easy to continuously be mindful of what you consume or to stay on top of meal prepping and cooking at home, rather than relying on instant meals or fast food.

Thankfully, there are plenty of available resources to help build and reinforce these habits, improving our nutrition. If you're interested in learning more, please visit our nutrition page to explore further!
